Business Activity

Clairmont Partners LLC is a consulting firm providing business strategy and commercial development advisory services to small and medium-sized businesses and entrepreneurs.

The company supports clients in structuring sales operations, improving commercial performance, and implementing practical growth plans.

How we get paid: services are billed as consulting projects or monthly advisory retainers. Payments are typically received via bank transfer from business clients.
